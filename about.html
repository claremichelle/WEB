About Me

This is some information about me.I'm Clare Michelle , a passionate web developer and designer based in Nairobi,Kenya.with over 7 years of experience in creating dynamic, expressive and beautiful web pages.I aim to deliver what is expected in quality.

Background

I grew up in a small village in Kirinyaga County.I was first introduced to computer when i was in form 1 at Loreto Limuru Highschool and my intrest grew and i was able to sit for the national examination and attained an A grade in ComputerStudies.I spent countless hours teaching myself how to code by building simple websites and games.This led me to pursue a degree on Software Development from KCA University thereby creating a strong foundation in coding.

What I Do

I specialize in front-end development especially JavaScript,HTML and CSS.I particularly enjoy transforming complex problems into beautifully sculpted designs.My goal and most utter joy is to create engaging, simple and accessible web experiencesthat meet both the needs of the business and customers needs.

Personal Projects

During my free time, I engage my self in personal projects that combine my interests in technology and transform it to earn an extra source of income.One of my most recent projects is known as KUJO and is accessible on playstore which is used to edit videos and photos which can assist content creaters to add more clarity to their videos for the interest of their followers.
